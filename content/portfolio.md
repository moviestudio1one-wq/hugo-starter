## Hardware Repairs

### Desktop System Recovery
**Challenge:** Client's gaming PC wouldn't start after a power surge.  
**Solution:** Diagnosed failed power supply and motherboard damage. Replaced components and ran full system diagnostics.  
**Result:** System restored to full functionality. Added surge protection devices to prevent future incidents.

### Laptop Screen & Logic Board Repair
**Challenge:** Laptop had flickering display and random shutdown issues.  
**Solution:** Identified failing LCD cable and overheating CPU. Replaced display cable and reapplied thermal paste.  
**Result:** Laptop running smoothly with improved performance and stability.

### Storage Drive Recovery
**Challenge:** Important business files lost after accidental drive format.  
**Solution:** Used professional data recovery techniques to retrieve files from unformatted drive.  
**Result:** 98% of data recovered. Implemented backup strategy for future protection.
