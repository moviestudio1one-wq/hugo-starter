## Hardware Repairs

### Critical Desktop System Recovery
**Challenge:** Client's gaming PC wouldn't start after a power surge destroyed components.  
**Symptoms:** No boot, smoke smell, power supply likely failed.  
**Solution:** 
- Tested power supply (confirmed failed)
- Inspected motherboard for damage (detected burned capacitors)
- Replaced power supply and motherboard
- Reinstalled OS and verified all hardware functioning
- Installed surge protector to prevent future incidents

**Result:** System fully restored to working condition. Client back online within 24 hours. System running smoothly 12+ months later.

---

### Laptop Display & Thermal Management
**Challenge:** Laptop had flickering display, random shutdowns, and overheating.  
**Symptoms:** Screen intermittently goes black, system crashes under load, very hot to touch.  
**Solution:**
- Diagnosed failing LCD display cable (physical damage)
- Identified thermal paste degradation on CPU
- Replaced display cable
- Reapplied professional thermal paste to CPU and GPU
- Cleaned internal cooling system
- Updated BIOS and drivers
- Ran stress tests to verify stability

**Result:** Laptop running reliably with 15-20°C temperature reduction. No more shutdowns. Display stable. System feels like new.

### Performance Crisis - 5-Minute Boot Times
**Challenge:** Business laptop running "incredibly slow" - boot time approaching 5+ minutes.  
**Impact:** Employee spending 30+ minutes daily waiting for system, lost productivity.  
**Solution:**
- Deep system analysis (found 50+ startup programs)
- Cleaned malware and PUP (potentially unwanted programs)
- Removed bloatware and unnecessary startup items
- Updated all drivers
- Replaced mechanical HDD with SSD (240GB)
- Optimized disk usage (freed 80GB space)
- Cleaned registry and temporary files
- Applied Windows optimization

**Result:** 
- Boot time reduced from 5 minutes to 45 seconds (85% improvement)
- System feels completely new
- Employee productivity increased significantly
- System remains fast 18+ months later

## Network & Connectivity Solutions

### WiFi Network Setup for Home Office
**Challenge:** Remote worker had constant WiFi disconnections and poor signal coverage.  
**Problem Areas:** Dead zones in office, frequent dropouts, slow speeds affecting video calls.  
**Solution:**
- Site survey to identify signal issues
- Analyzed interference (neighboring networks, appliances)
- Optimized router placement
- Configured mesh network with 2 additional access points
- Secured WiFi with strong encryption
- Optimized WiFi channels and settings
- Tested coverage in all office areas

**Result:** 
- Full WiFi coverage throughout home office
- Zero dropouts (99.8% uptime)
- Consistent speeds 50+ Mbps
- Video calls now stable and reliable
